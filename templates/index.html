Coming 6.1.2021 — Al's Magic Cosmic Takes

Machine Tone presents its very first release, Al's Magic Cosmic Takes. It's an ecstatic, ideosyncratic and funked-out love affair with 90's US electro. If you like Robert Hood, Drexciya, Dopplereffekt, we think you'll like this.

Music written, recorded and mixed by Alex Ramsdell. Mastered by Dietrich Schoenemann.
