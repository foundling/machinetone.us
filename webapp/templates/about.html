Machine Tone is a small label run by two friends, Alex Ramsdell and Justin Reed, looking to carve out a space to showcase their evolving music. Alex is a multi-instrumentalist, recording musician and software developer living in Western Massachusetts. Justin is a veteran Chicago DJ and experimental musician living in Woburn, MA. They met at the Boston Modular Synthesizers meetup and soon after Machine Tone was born.
